# Portfolio

🎨 **Live site:** [maazahmedd.github.io/portfolio](https://maazahmedd.github.io/portfolio/)

A personal portfolio site built for NYU Abu Dhabi's **Communications Lab** class, showcasing a semester's worth of creative web and media projects. The site is a static HTML/CSS/JavaScript build with a color-picker theme, an "a day in my life" data visualization, and a work section linking to four collaborative projects.

## Featured projects

### Connections — The Movie (30 Minute Film Festival)
A branding and showcase site for a short film made for NYUAD's 30 Minute Film Festival. The theme was "connections" — how the pandemic reshaped the way we connect with what we love. Built with Kyle Adams and Jeongin Lee.
- Live: <https://maazahmedd.github.io/>
- Source: [maazahmedd.github.io](https://github.com/maazahmedd/maazahmedd.github.io)

### Year of the 50th — The UAE Comic
An interactive scrolling web comic covering the history of the United Arab Emirates, from the nomadic tribes of the region all the way to the celebration of the 50th National Day. Built with Kyle Adams and Cole Beasley.
- Live: <https://maazahmedd.github.io/comm-lab-comic/>
- Source: [comm-lab-comic](https://github.com/maazahmedd/comm-lab-comic)

### Mental Health Oasis
An auditive safe space inspired by the animated documentary *My Depression*. Four animated landscapes tied together by a narrator's reflections on mental health, designed to let users disconnect from noise and focus on the sounds of nature.
- Live: <https://maazahmedd.github.io/mental-health-oasis/>
- Source: [mental-health-oasis](https://github.com/maazahmedd/mental-health-oasis)

### Your Student is Late to Class — Lost Student
An interactive video narrative where the user, playing the professor, determines the sequence of events leading up to Mehani's late arrival to class. Built with Thais Alvarenga, Riley Ha, and Chinonyerem Ukaegbu.
- Live: <https://maazahmedd.github.io/loststudent/>
- Source: [loststudent](https://github.com/maazahmedd/loststudent)
